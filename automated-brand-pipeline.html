Technical Challenge & Business Problem

Enterprise Challenge

Modern businesses need hundreds of marketing assets across multiple campaigns, platforms, and seasonal variations. Traditional design workflows create bottlenecks:

  • Design team capacity: Limited to 10-15 assets per week
  • Cost scalability: $200-500 per asset with designer time
  • Brand consistency: Manual variations lead to 30% brand compliance issues
  • Campaign velocity: 2-3 week turnaround prevents rapid market response

Technical Requirements

  • Automated asset generation with brand consistency enforcement
  • Multi-modal AI integration (text, image, video generation)
  • Quality control workflows with systematic validation
  • Scalable processing for batch operations
  • Cost optimization through systematic resource management
